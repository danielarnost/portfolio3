Background

I became interested in web development as a graduate student, during which time I manipulated code in MATLAB and COMSOL for my thesis. I was able to use data I gathered to plug into the models, but I wanted to learn how write this code from scratch and manipulate scientific models to fit the needs of my research and my laboratory. After completing my degree I wanted to keep learning and my primary focus shifted from geology to web development. I have a B.S. in Geosciences from University of Massachusetts, Amherst as well as an M.S. in Geosciences from The University of Texas at Austin. My ideal position would integrate past experience with new web development skills and I am always open to learning new and challenging languages and technologies.
